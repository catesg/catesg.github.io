Research

When we greet, we connect. In the classroom, the connection, or lack thereof, between student and teacher can affect the way a student learns and a classroom functions. Proactive Classroom Management strategies, such a Positive Greeting at the Door (PGD), aim to create and develop that connection to positively and proactively manage class behaviour. My current research is looking to determine if PGD not only aids in increasing academic engagement time, but if it affects students' feelings of wellbeing within the classroom.
