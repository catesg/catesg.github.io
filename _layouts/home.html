Why Positive Communication?

Positive communication practices promote well-being and emotional intelligence within individuals, teams and organisations. By understanding and improving the way we communicate, we can strengthen relationships, reduce misunderstandings, and become clearer about what we want. We make our interactions and our relationships with others truly count; in both our professional and personal lives.

Schools & Learning Institutions

Student well-being is an important part of developing socially conscious and globally-minded individuals ready to take their place in the wider world. Well-being and mental health underpin academic achievement and prepares our young people to lead rich, purposeful lives.
Learning to communicate well allows young people to understand others and express themselves as they move towards their goals.

Teams & Organisations

Positive communication and education practices enhance teamwork and productivity. Emotionally intelligent organisations that communicate well are more productive, dynamic and happier places to work. It can attract great candidates and reduce staff turnover. Help your organisation stand out as a place people want to do business in, and business with.

About Me

Communication is one of the most important life skills, yet many of us do not get the best out of our communication interactions; whether speaking, questioning, or listening. This can lead to untapped potential and missed opportunities.

I’m Cate Stewart-Gifford, a teacher, trainer and communication professional with over 25 years of experience in communication roles. As a qualified teacher I have trained and taught students at both elementary and secondary level and am a certified Vocational Trainer of Adult Learners (EQF) with the Human Resources Development Authority in Cyprus.

I have written and delivered courses, workshops, and webinars on topics such as career guidance, language use in the classroom, behaviour and influence, reading literacy for well-being, and positive communication.

The training and support I offer focuses on positive communication practices to strengthen individuals, teams and organisations.

What is Positive Communication?

Positive Communication is an approach to communication that helps us understand, deepen, and strengthen the ways in which we communicate. It increases the value that communication brings to our sense of self, our relationships, the way we work, and the way we live.
